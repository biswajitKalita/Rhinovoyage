Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

This Refund Policy details the refund process, eligibility guidelines, and timelines for bookings processed through RhinoVoyage. We are dedicated to ensuring a transparent refund process for all our travelers in Assam and the Northeast.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are initiated only for cancellations that comply with our active Cancellation Policy:

  • Compliant Cancellations: Fully eligible for refunds if cancelled within the free cancellation windows (24+ hours for vehicle rentals, 7+ days for tour packages).
  • System Cancellations: If a trip is cancelled by RhinoVoyage due to guide shortages, vehicle maintenance issues, or road blockages, the traveler is eligible for a 100% refund.
  • Disaster Cancellations: Cancellations triggered by unexpected landslides, river floods, or local political bandhs are fully eligible for refunds or free rescheduling credits.

⚠️ Processing Timeline: All approved refund requests are processed within 5 to 7 business days. Funds are credited back using the payment details provided at the time of claim validation.

2. Processing Methods

Since booking payments are flexible, refunds are settled using the following payment systems:

  • UPI Accounts: Direct transfer to the user's UPI handle (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM). This is the fastest method.
  • Bank Accounts: IMPS/NEFT bank transfer. Travelers must provide their bank name, account holder name, account number, and IFSC code to coordinate the transaction.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

RhinoVoyage is unable to provide refunds in the following circumstances:

  • No-shows where the traveler fails to arrive at the pickup location without notifying our support desk.
  • Mid-trip changes or cancellations requested by the traveler once the tour has commenced.
  • Refusal of service by the driver due to traveler misconduct, illegal activity, or failure to present valid identity proof at the time of boarding.
  • Government permit fees (such as ILPs for Arunachal Pradesh or entrance fees for Kaziranga Wildlife Safari) once they have already been processed and issued.

4. Disputed Claims

For any disputes regarding cancellation charges or refund amounts, please contact our dispute resolution team at rhinovoyage@gmail.com with your booking ID and receipts. We review all disputes on a case-by-case basis and aim to resolve them within 3 business days.
