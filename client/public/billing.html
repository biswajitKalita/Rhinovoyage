GST & Billing Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Welcome to the RhinoVoyage GST and Billing Guidelines page. We maintain clear invoicing standards and comply with the GST regulations of the Government of India. This statement outlines our tax structures, payment options, and requirements for corporate travelers claiming Input Tax Credit (ITC).

1. Service Pricing & Estimates

All prices listed on our website, advertisements, and itinerary proposals are initial estimates based on standard routes and seasonal packages starting from Sivasagar. The final invoice amount will reflect:

  • The actual distance traveled (if car rentals exceed the initial day package limits).
  • Tolls, state entry permits (e.g. for Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh), and parking charges (unless explicitly bundled).
  • The applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST) rate.

2. Applicable GST Rates

In accordance with Indian tax rules for transport and tour operators:

  • Car Rental / Passenger Transport: Billed at 5% GST under CGST/SGST (without Input Tax Credit on inputs), or as defined by specific vehicle classifications.
  • Tour Package Operations: Billed at 5% GST as a composite tour package operator service.

💼 Corporate Bookings: If you are booking travel for corporate business operations and require Input Tax Credit (ITC), please provide your company details during the booking request flow.

3. Input Tax Credit (ITC) Claims

To receive a GST-compliant invoice that enables your company to claim Input Tax Credit, you must provide the following details at the time of booking submission or final settlement:

  • Registered Company Name
  • Company GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number)
  • Corporate Billing Address
  • Company Contact Email and Phone Number

Please note that retrospectively modifying billing details or adding GSTINs after an invoice has been closed and uploaded to the GST portal is not possible.

4. Accepted Payment Methods

We support a variety of payment methods to keep bookings flexible:

  • UPI Applications: Instant transfers via GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM UPI.
  • Bank Transfers: NEFT, RTGS, or IMPS for tour packages and corporate accounts.
  • Cash: Cash payments are accepted directly by drivers upon completion of rentals or during tour operations.

5. Invoicing & Delivery

RhinoVoyage automatically generates digital invoices for every trip. A receipt copy is dispatched directly to the traveler's registered email address and WhatsApp number within 24 hours of trip completion and payment clearance. For paper invoices, please coordinate with our Sivasagar main office.
