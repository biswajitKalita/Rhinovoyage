The Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: The Seats of Power

09:00 AM

Rang Ghar

Start at Asia's oldest amphitheater. Brief history: Used by Ahom Kings to watch buffalo fights and sports.

11:30 AM

Talatal Ghar

Explore the multi-storied palace with its famous underground tunnels.

01:30 PM

Traditional Assamese Lunch

A stop at a handpicked local eatery for a Paramparagat (traditional) Thali.

03:30 PM

Joysagar Pukhuri & Joy Dol

Visit the largest man-made tank in India, dedicated to Sati Joymoti.

Evening

Check-in

Check-in to your selected hotel and relax.

Day 2: Spirituality & Architecture

08:00 AM

Shiva Dol, Vishnu Dol & Devi Dol

Witness the morning prayers at the tallest Shiva temple in India.

11:00 AM

Gargaon Palace (Kareng Ghar)

A short drive to the seven-storied architectural marvel.

02:00 PM

Charaideo Maidam

Visit the majestic royal burial grounds of the Ahom Kings, often called the Pyramids of Assam.
